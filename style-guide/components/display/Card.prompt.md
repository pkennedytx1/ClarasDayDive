Rounded paper surface with a warm soft shadow — the container for menu items, events, tiles.

```jsx
<Card interactive>
  <Eyebrow>Patio</Eyebrow>
  <h3>Open till midnight</h3>
</Card>
<Card tone="rose" padding="lg">…</Card>
```
Tones: `white | cream | rose | teal | ink`. Padding `none | sm | md | lg`. `interactive` adds hover-lift.
